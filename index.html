From Crystal to Current.

We begin by extracting Synaptium crystals, a powerful natural resource. These crystals are then used to create high-capacity batteries, power advanced technologies for the extraction of deep-sea resources, and generate electricity for broader use. Once their energy is depleted, the crystals are returned to the ocean, where they naturally recharge over time—allowing the cycle to begin again. This closed-loop system ensures sustainable energy use and minimal environmental impact.
