The Hydra


By year 2027, we aim to:
> Switch to 100% renewable energy for internal processes.
> Eliminate all single-use plastics from operations.

By year 2030, we aim to:
> Launch fully circular product lines (No waste just reuse)
> Implement zero-waste protocols at all manufacturing and distribution sites.

By year 2035, we aim to:
> Achieve 100% material traceability and accountability.
> Lead national efforts in low-impact innovation research.

By year 2040, we aim to:
> Be recognised as the worlds first fully regenerative design company.
> Achieve global certification in next gen biomaterial leadership.
