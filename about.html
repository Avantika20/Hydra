About Hydra

What’s Hydra All About?

We here at Hydra are passionate about building the next generation of ocean infrastructure—a field thus far, left largely untouched. Powered by Synaptium—a compact, high efficiency energy core, our Aquanodes navigate the seafloor with GPS accuracy to harvest critical minerals and convert seawater into potable water using pressure adaptive filtration. Designed for longevity and scalability, each unit is autonomous, remotely monitored, and engineered to function in some of the planet’s most extreme conditions. With every drop and data point, Hydra is redefining what’s possible underwater.
